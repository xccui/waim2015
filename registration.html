WAIM 2015 Registration for International Participants

Full/Student Registration includes admission to all WAIM 2015 conference and workshop sessions, the conference proceedings, coffee breaks, conference banquet, lunches and dinners.

You can pick up the official receipt for the registration during the conference.

For paper authors

  • For each accepted paper, at least one author must register at the full rate, even if all authors are students. Completed registration and presentation are required for each accepted paper to be included in the conference proceedings.
  • Authors of each accepted main conference paper must complete the full registration before April 20, 2015. Authors of each accepted Workshop paper must complete the full registration before May 10, 2015.
  • If you have multiple accepted papers, each paper must have a separate full registration.
