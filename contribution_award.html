Hongjun Lu WAIM Outstanding Contribution Award

We are happy to announce that this year's "Hongjun Lu WAIM Outstanding Contribution Award" will be given to Prof. Shan Wang from Renmin University!

Prof. Shan Wang was nominated by the WAIM award selection committee and got unanimous support from the WAIM steering committee. WAIM outstanding contribution award was set up since last year. The award was set up for individuals who have made outstanding contributions to WAIM. Prof. Shan Wang was nominated due to her great effort in promoting and supporting WAIM during her tenure as the Chair of the CCF TCDB. The award ceremony will be held in WAIM banquet and Prof. Shan Wang will give a speech about her experience in her career, vision to the future of the community.
