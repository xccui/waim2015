Call For Participation

The 16th International Conference on Web-Age Information Management (WAIM2015)
http://www.cs.sdu.edu.cn/waim2015/
June 8-10, 2015, Qingdao, Shandong, China
The organizing committee is excited to invite you to register and participate in the WAIM 2015 Conference. WAIM is a premier international conference for researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to share and exchange ideas, results, experience, techniques, and tools in connection with all aspects of data management. As the 16th event in the increasingly popular series, WAIM2015 expects to attract participants from all over the world to Qingdao, a beautiful coastal city in north China, and continues to establish WAIM as one of the major conferences on Web-based information systems.

CONFERENCE AND WORKSHOP DATES

Conference dates: June 9-10, 2015
Workshops: June 8, 2015

CONFERENCE LOCATION

Qingdao Garden Hotel, Qingdao, China

HIGHLIGHTS

- Keynotes

WAIM 2015 will feature three keynote speeches from world-renowned scholars:

  • Cipherbase: End-to-end Encryption for SQL, by Donald Kossmann, Microsoft
  • Towards a Unified Platform for Semantically-related Data, by Michael Benedikt, Oxford University
  • Data Cleaning, Linking, and Integration: From Raw Data to Actionable Information, by Wang-Chiew Tan, University of California, Santa Cruz

- Distinguished Young Lecturer (DYL) Series

In addition, WAIM 2015 will host three invited talks as part of the Distinguished Young Lecturer series:
  • Scale-independent query answering on big data, by Floris Geerts, University of Antwerp
  • Social Media Mining and Analysis for Business and Financial Innovation, by Feida Zhu, Singapore Management University
  • Natural Language Question Answering Over Knowledge Graph--A Data-driven Approach, by Lei Zou, Peking University

- Research sessions, posters, and demos

With 33 full papers, the research sessions will cover a variety of exciting topics including graph & social network, information & knowledge, big data, and recommender systems. This year, all the research papers are presented in single-track sessions to foster more lively and interdisciplinary discussions. There will be 32 posters and 6 demos as well.
For a quick look at the program, please see Program At a Glance.

- WAIM/SAP Summer School

The WAIM/SAP Summer School will be held in conjunction with the WAIM 2015 conference from June 7 to June 10. Launched in 2011, WAIM summer schools comprises intensive courses which aim to provide attendees with the latest developments in database-related fields. This year, we have three tutorials and three distinguished invited talks that cover a variety of topics. Please see http://59.108.48.38/waim2015/index.htm for more information.

Looking forward to seeing you in Qingdao!
