Call For Workshops

WAIM is a leading international conference for researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to share and exchange ideas, results, experiences, techniques, and tools in connection with all aspects of Web data management. As the 16th event in the increasingly popular series, WAIM2015 expects to attract participants from all over the world to Qingdao, China, and continues to establish WAIM as one of the major conferences on Web-based information systems.

We solicit proposals for half-day or full-day workshops in conjunction with WAIM 2015. Workshops are expected to focus on novel ideas on current and emerging topics relevant to the conference. All papers accepted by WAIM 2015 workshops will be published in a combined volume of Lecture Notes in Computer Science series published by Springer.

All workshops will benefit from the registration process of WAIM 2015 (we will have a single registration for conferences, workshops and tutorials). The workshop organizer(s) will be responsible for advertising the workshop, reviewing and selecting the papers.

Important Dates

Workshop proposal due:Feb. 21, 2015 (11:59pm PST)
Notification:Feb. 28, 2015

Submission Instructions

Workshop proposals should include the following information:

  • Workshop title and acronym
  • Brief description about the workshop, including a list of topics
  • Names and contact information for the organizer(s)
  • Proposed duration of the workshop (half day or full day)
  • Potential PC members for the workshop
  • Expected number of attendees
  • Brief biography of the organizer(s)

Proposals and enquiries should be sent to waim2015workshops@gmail.com with the subject: "WAIM 2015 Workshop Proposal".
